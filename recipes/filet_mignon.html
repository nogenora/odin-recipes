Filet Mignon

This is the most fabulous fish soup recipe you are ever gonna taste !

You won't believe it!

Ingredients:

  • fish, whatever fish you like most
  • tomatoes, red is better
  • carrots
  • chilly pepper

Steps:

  1. - slice the onions, garlic, the leak and cook in butter.
  2. - add the fish, the tomatoes and carrots...
  3. - Then add spices and cover with water
  4. - Finally add some white wine, then level up with water and cover. The soup should simmer not boil.
